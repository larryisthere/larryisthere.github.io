# Dawnary - Support Information

## App Overview

Dawnary is a mobile application focused on personal diary recording. We are committed to providing you with a minimalist and healing writing space, allowing you to easily record every moment in your life.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How to Use Dawnary?

1. **Start Recording**: After opening the app, tap the "+" button to create a new diary entry
2. **Edit Content**: Supports rich text editing, you can insert images and format text
3. **Auto-Save**: The app will automatically save when you switch to background or close the app
4. **View History**: On the main page, you can view all diary entries by date

### Where is Data Stored?

- **Local Storage**: All diary content is stored locally on your device by default
- **iCloud Backup (Optional)**: You can enable iCloud backup in settings to sync data to your other devices

### How to Enable iCloud Backup?

1. Open app settings
2. Find the "iCloud Backup" option
3. Turn on backup
4. The first sync may take some time, please be patient

### How to Delete a Diary Entry?

In the diary list, swipe left on a diary entry and tap the "Delete" button to delete it.

### How to Insert Images?

1. On the edit page, tap the image icon on the keyboard toolbar
2. Choose "Select from Photo Library" or "Take Photo"
3. The image will be automatically inserted at the current cursor position

### How is Location Information Used?

- Location recording is optional
- If enabled, the app records the city name where you create diary entries (city-level only)
- Location information is only used to display city names in diary entries to enhance context
- Location information is stored only locally on your device and is not uploaded to any servers
- You can disable location recording at any time in settings

### Does the App Require Internet Connection?

No. Dawnary's core functionality is completely offline and works without an internet connection. Network connection is only needed when iCloud backup is enabled for data synchronization.

### How to Export or Share Diary Entries?

1. On the diary edit page, tap the menu button in the top right corner
2. Select "Generate Share Image"
3. The app will convert diary content to image format
4. You can save to photo library or share directly
